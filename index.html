About Global Pack

Built on quality,
proven by history

From a single converting line in Cairo to a full-scale flexible packaging operation, Global Pack has grown by doing one thing consistently: delivering packaging that meets specification, on time, every time. Based in Badr Industrial City, we combine advanced printing, lamination, and pouch-making with an unwavering commitment to certified quality.

Capabilities

What we manufacture

Flexible Packaging

Laminates, pouches, sachets and roll-stock films engineered for barrier performance and shelf appeal.

Advanced Printing

Up to 10-colour flexo and rotogravure printing with lamination, cold seal and matte/gloss finishing.

Pouch Manufacturing

Stand-up pouches, flat-bottom bags, spouted pouches and specialty formats for every segment.

Lamination & Converting

Multi-layer film structures optimised for barrier, heat-seal, and sustainability requirements.

Design & Prepress

Artwork adaptation, structural design, and colour management from concept to production-ready files.

Sustainable Solutions

Recyclable, mono-material and compostable packaging options meeting modern environmental standards.
